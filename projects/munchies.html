Munchies Fast Food

Munchies fast food is a fictional fast food restaurant created for a class project in early 2017. The fictional restaurant, located outside our school,  had issues handling the customer traffic during major meal times and required a solution. My group members and I created this online menu and  booking system to help solve the problem as customers could now book their meals from class or their hostels and choose to have it brought to them or be alerted when it is ready for pick up. All data from orders is saved in a database in the cloud and can be exported in various formats.
